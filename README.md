MSP 
is a MATLAB-based tool for advanced analysis of particulate matter from Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) images. It offers researchers and engineers a fast, reliable, and precise workflow to detect, measure, and characterize particle morphology.

Key Features
- Automatic Particle Detection: Identify individual particles in SEM images quickly and accurately.
- Morphological Characterization: Extract particle size, shape, and structural parameters for comprehensive quantitative analysis.
- High-Resolution Support: Compatible with a wide range of SEM image resolutions and formats.
- Validated Accuracy: MSP results were carefully compared with ImageJ, showing minimal differences and demonstrating high reliability.
- Data-Driven Insights: Generates metrics and visualizations that facilitate statistical analysis, reporting, and reproducibility.
- Customizable Workflow: Flexible MATLAB scripts allow adaptation to diverse research needs and sample types.

Why MSP?

MSP transforms raw SEM images into actionable scientific data efficiently and reliably. Its validation against ImageJ confirms that users can trust MSP’s measurements, making it a powerful tool for studies in air quality, materials science, nanotechnology, and beyond.

By automating labor-intensive image analysis while ensuring accuracy and reproducibility, MSP accelerates research and provides confidence in the results, bridging the gap between imaging and insight.
